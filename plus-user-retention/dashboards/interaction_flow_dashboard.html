Key Takeaways

What This Tells Us

FindingImplicationNext Step
76% non-player rate, 68.5% spend 10+ min browsing Users navigate but don't start games. Conversion-to-play barrier, not awareness. Screen-level analysis: what screens do non-players visit? Where do they get stuck?
Trial non-players 82% vs Direct 65% Trial users convert through paywall but many never intended to use the app. "Accidental trials." Segment trial non-players: <2 min + 0 games = likely forced paywall conversion
Bimodal engagement: 0 or 11+ games No gradual ramp-up. Users who start playing get hooked; the problem is starting. Focus on the 0→1 transition, not improving existing player experience
Multifeed = 71% of 1st games The main feed is the primary entry point. Its content and ordering matter most for activation. Analyze multifeed position: does feed order affect first-game choice?
feed_set_topic: 0.1% completion Effectively broken source. 3.5K users enter but almost none complete a game. Bug or UX issue? Needs investigation with product team.
42% of churned leave after completing They didn't fail. They just didn't come back. Missing re-engagement hook. Post-game experience: what do users see after completing? Is there a "next" prompt?
